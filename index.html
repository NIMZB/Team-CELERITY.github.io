About Team Celerity


Team celerity is a team that focuses on the future. We believe that Solar energy is the technology of the future and thats why we focus on manufacturing a solar electric hybrid car.


Our goal is to provide a sustainable mode of transport for the people of the future. This challenge requires us to design a car that is capable of overcoming the day to day challenges faced on the roads while also providing a comfortable way of travelling. Our passion for sustainable development fuels our actions as we work hard towards our goal.


There are many challenges along the way, but they are overcome by our experience and knowledge of fundamental concepts. Our motto 'Innovate to Sustain' is what drives us to tackle these challenges head on and emerge victorious.

The Team


The team consists of hardworking engineering student who come together to pool their knowledge on various desciplines like electrical, electronics and mechanical engineering to design and manufacture a solar electric hybrid car within the constraint of time and money.


A feat that requires immense passion and dedication towards their team and their task. Everyday spending 4 hours in the workshop working slowly and steadily towards their goal of competing in national level competitions.


Guided by our experienced seniors, we go through intense rounds of brainstorming to finalize the perfect design for our car to provide the best performance and comfort to the driver. This process tests our designing and creative thinking capabilities and we give our 100% to beat any challenges faced by us.
